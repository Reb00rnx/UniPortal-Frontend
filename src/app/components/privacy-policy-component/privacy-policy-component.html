Privacy Policy

This policy describes how the University Portal manages and protects your data. As an educational project, we prioritize transparency and security.

1. Data We Collect

  • Personal Information: First name, last name, index number, role.
  • Authentication Data: E-mail address and hashed passwords.
  • Academic Data: Course enrollment and academic grades.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Authorize access via JWT.
  • Handle course and grade management logic.
  • Generate academic performance reports.

3. Security Measures

  • Password Hashing: Passwords are never stored in plain text.
  • Stateless Security: Protected by Spring Security filters.

4. Contact

Use the Contact tab for any questions regarding this project.
